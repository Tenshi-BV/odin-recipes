Boiled water

Description

It's finally here! The one and only way how to prepare a water, that is boiling!

This never-seen-before classic has finally arrived nowhere! Here's how to get your paws on some of it.

Ingredients:

  • water
  • sandwich
  • 200ml carton drink packages bought in bulk

Steps:

  1. Create a cast pickaxe head.
  2. Combine it with a wooden stick to create a handle.
  3. Go outside and start mining for a water well.
  4. Take regular brakes and consume your government-provided food, sandwaches and softdrinks, for nutrition.
  5. Once you acquire water from a well, use your electrical water boiler in your kitchen to boil it in about a minute.
