Peeled potato

Description

How to guide on the making of a peeled potato.

Content warning for the Irish.

Ingredients:

  • potato
  • water
  • salt(optional)

Steps:

  1. Put your potatoes in a body of water
  2. Let them soak for a while, preferebly a couple of hours
  3. Take a potato out of the water
  4. Use a peeler, or a knife if you don't have one, to scrape the skin off of a potato.
  5. For difficult areas or indents, use a knife.
